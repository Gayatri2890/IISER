International Relationships in Higher Education

The International Relations Office (IRO) at IISER was established in the year 2014 to facilitate internationalisation efforts across campus with specific emphasis on the academic collaborations and bilateral exchanges between IISER Pune and foreign institutions/universities. The IRO works pro-actively to develop and maintain international partnerships. It aims to build IISER Pune as a global educational brand, a leader in research and education.
